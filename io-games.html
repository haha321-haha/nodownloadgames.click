What are IO Games?

IO games are browser-based multiplayer games where players compete against each other in real-time. The name "IO" comes from the .io domain extension, which was popularized by games like Agar.io. These games are known for their simple controls, competitive gameplay, and the ability to play with people from around the world without downloading anything.
