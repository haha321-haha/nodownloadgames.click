Battle Royale

Fight against players worldwide in this intense battle royale game. Collect weapons, build defenses, and be the last one standing!

About Battle Royale

Battle Royale is an intense multiplayer game where 100 players drop onto an island, scavenge for weapons and resources, and fight to be the last person standing. The playable area gradually shrinks, forcing players into closer encounters.

Game Controls

  • W Move forward
  • S Move backward
  • A Move left
  • D Move right
  • Space Jump
  • Shift Run
  • E Interact/Pick up items
  • Left Click Shoot
  • Right Click Aim
  • R Reload
  • Tab Open inventory
  • M Open map

Tips & Tricks

  • Land away from the center to avoid early confrontations
  • Always check your surroundings before looting
  • Use headphones to hear enemy footsteps
  • Build defensive structures when under fire
  • Stay within the safe zone to avoid taking damage
  • Use vehicles to quickly move between locations
  • Stock up on healing items for the final battles
