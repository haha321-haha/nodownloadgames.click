Game Rules

  • Use the arrow keys to move the block.
  • The up arrow rotates the block.
  • The down arrow accelerates the block.
  • On mobile devices, use the on-screen buttons.
  • When a row is filled, it is removed and you earn points.
  • The more rows you clear, the higher your score.
  • As you progress, the block will fall faster.
  • When the block reaches the top, the game ends.
  • Press spacebar or click "Pause" to pause the game.
