Game Rules

  • Click on a card to flip it, and you can flip two cards at once.
  • If two cards match, they will remain flipped.
  • If two cards don't match, they will automatically flip back.
  • The goal is to find all matching pairs with the fewest steps.
  • You can choose different difficulty levels to increase or decrease the number of cards.
  • "Hint" button can temporarily show all cards, but it will increase step count.
  • When all matching pairs are found, the game ends.
